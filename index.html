SCalimero

Scalimero is a Scala wrapper library for Calimero. Its main propose is to be a concise alternative to Calimero. Additionally a DSL was included to ease testing and learning of KNX devices.
Calimero is a collection of Java APIs that together form a foundation for building applications controlling EIB/KNX installations. Detailed knowledge of the protocol is not required. On the top of that Calimero only requires J2ME environment, which enables use on embedded platforms. It is an open source project and was developed by the Institute of Computer Aided Automation of the Vienna University of Technology.

Features:

  1. DSL for scripting, testing and playing in the REPL
  2. Wrappers for all Datapoints and DPTs
  3. Bounds of values are checked
  4. The right types apear were they should(no more useless casting)
  5. Actor support
  6. Abstractions for Grouping of Devices

Install

Dependencies: Scala 2.8.0 with JRE and calimero 2.0a4 or higher

To use Scalimero in one of your projects, just install Scala 2.8.0 with JRE and add Calimero and Scalimero to your classpath.
If you want to contribute or fork Scalimero we advise you to choose sbt for building. All configurations have already been done. After starting sbt you just have to type "update" and all depencys will be downloaded automatically.
